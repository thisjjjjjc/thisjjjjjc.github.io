Chen crafts cross-medium interactive experiences to explore how we can better express, understand, and feel connected with ourselves, others, and the world. Recently, she has been interested in exploring metaphors as design methods for integrating graphics, sound, music, text, and interactivity. Her workflow combines 2D/3D illustration using Procreate and Blender, sound and music design using Logic Pro X, and blends all asset ingredients in Unity for interactivity.
